Hush Puppies

Description

This delicious recipe is made not with deep frying, but with an air fryer. Only being a bit denser in texture, they're perfect for other dishes as a side!

Can be served with fried shrimp, or fried fish - or they're equally good with a drizzle of honey.

Ingredients

  • Non-fat cooking spray
  • 1 cup Yellow Cornmeal
  • 3/4 cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon Cayenne Pepper, or more to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 2 tablespoons minced onion
  • 2 tablespoons mineced green bell pepper
  • 3/4 cup Low-Fat Buttermilk
  • 1 large egg

Steps

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 390 degrees F (198 degrees C) according to a manufacturer's insturctions. Line the bottom of the air fryer basket with aluminum foil and spray with nonfat cooking spray.
  2. Stir cornmeal, flour, baking powder, salt, cayenne, and garlic powderr together in a large bowl. Mix onoin and bell pepper.
  3. Whisk buttermilk and egg together in a separate bowl. Stir into cornmeal mixture. Let mixture rest for 5 minutes.
  4. Cook in the preheated air fryer until golden brown, cripsy, and cooked through, 9 to 10 minutes. Serve hot.
